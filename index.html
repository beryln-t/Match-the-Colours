Welcome to match the colours where the objective of the game is, you've guessed it, match the colours!

To win, complete matching all the colours before the timer runs out. But wait there's a catch! For each wrong match a life will be deducted, so choose wisely. To start, click on the "Start" button, then click on any tiles to begin matching.
